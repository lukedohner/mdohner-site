Exhibition Designer and Project Manager

I collaborate with clients to develop their exhibit ideas into exciting, buildable plans. As exhibit designer, I create gallery layouts, construction docs, models, and comprehensive design packages, including graphics. As project manager, I help clients plan, test, and coordinate the installation of their exhibits.

Design clients: National Public Housing Museum • Chinese-American Museum of Chicago • The Brinton Museum, Big Horn, Wyoming • Field Museum • Chicago Collections Consortium • Wilmette Historical Museum • Campbell Center for Historical Preservation Studies
